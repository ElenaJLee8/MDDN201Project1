Internet Infrastructure of Wellington

Above our heads and beneath our feet is a network connecting us to the internet at any given moment in time.
Manhole covers, CCTV cameras, antennae, and markings on the street
are just some of the physical ways we can see the internet around us.

Manhole Covers

One of a few ways that we can get a glimpse of the networks below us, manhole covers come in many different sizes and designs. Pipe systems and a series of cables are hidden under these covers, sending data all over Wellington and connecting us all. They are usually marked with the names of the companies that own them, a few being Hynds Pipe Systems, Humes Pipeline Systems and the Wellington City Council.
