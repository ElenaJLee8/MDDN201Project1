Internet Infrastructure of Wellington

Above our heads and beneath our feet is a network connecting us to the internet at any given moment in time.
Manhole covers, CCTV cameras, antennae, and markings on the street
are just some of the physical ways we can see the internet around us.

CCTV Cameras

There are CCTV cameras all around us, ranging from ones to monitor traffic, theft prevention and personal security. We as individuals are captured in many of these cameras every day without even thinking about it. Unwittingly we become a part of a wider connected network.
